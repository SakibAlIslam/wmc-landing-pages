Joyful celebrations driven by community and observance

Ramadan is a month of fasting and reflection among the Muslim Canadian community. Daily routines shift toward sunset and nighttime, with an increase in family gatherings, meal preparation, and giving. Eid al-Fitr is the celebration that marks the end of Ramadan, a joyful day of communal prayers, sharing meals, gift giving, and togetherness. ​
